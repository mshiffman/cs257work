Welcome to the Air Quality, GDP, and Population Explorer!

Are you interested in air quality, GDP, population, and/or their relationships? This program allows you to easily gain information and visually inspect the geological distributions of air quality, GDP, population, and the relationships between those variables in the US at the county level.

Ways to Explore the Data:

  • View the relationship between GDP, air quality, and/or population
  • Sort counties by type of data of your choosing (View top list of 1-10 counties)
  • Look up air quality data based on location(s)
  • View visual representations of data
